    EditNintendo Direct Sept 1 2016

    During a Nintendo Direct, it was revealed that Mario Maker would be coming to the Nintendo 3DS on December 2, 2016. While essentially a port of the Wii U version of the game, there are a few changes, which you can view in the 3DS FAQ.

    Super Mario Maker is a 2-D game that allows the players to create their own Mario levels using the Wii U GamePad. Drag and drop blocks, pipes, and all types of enemies from the top of the screen into the level; it's that easy!

    EditHow It Works

    Playtest the game immediately by double tapping the screen. If something isn't working out, return to edit mode and fix it.

     

    With the tap of an icon, users can even switch the visual style between a classic 8-bit Super Mario Bros., Super Mario Bros. 3 or Super Mario World look as well as the HD world of New Super Mario Bros. U.
