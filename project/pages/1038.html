    Field Manuals

    Edit Page    Last Edit: November 13, 2016 - 25 days 18 hours ago

    This is the guide to Battlefield 1's Field Manuals. Here you'll find the locations of every Field Manual in each chapter.

    There are a total of 66 Field Manuals in Battlefield 1. Field Manuals are hidden in chests.

    Storm of Steel Through Mud and Blood
    Friends in High Places Avanti Savoia!
    The Runner Nothing is Written

    EditStorm of Steel

    There are no Field Manuals in this story.

    EditThrough Mud and Blood

    There are 20 Field Manuals across this story's four chapters.

    EditOver the Top

    This chapter has five field manuals. You can start collecting them once you're allowed to exit the tank. 

    1. The first Field Manual is in the combat area just after you fix the tank. Drive up the hill and clear that circular combat area. Clear out the enemies, hop out, and walk toward the green. You'll find the crate with the collectible to the right of the green tent in rubble.

     

    The next two Field Manuals are in the tall building by the two sets of artillery. 

    2. Go behind the church and walk past the first door. You'll see a pile of junk in the corner of the outer wall. There's a bunch of crates and a big, green gear. The crate with the second Field Manual is there.

    3. This Field Manual is very close to the previous one. Walk into the building, and take an immediate right when you enter. Walk to the back wall and you'll see the crate with the Field Manual on the left.

      

    4. After you capture the last point hop out of the tank. Walk across to the building on the right and you'll find the crate with the Field Manual on the front left corner of the building.

    5. The last collectible is just before the bridge that ends the level. Walk up the road after fighting the tank a vehicle on the right side of the road. Walk around it and you'll see the crate with the Field Manual.

       


    EditFog of war

    The Fog of War chapter has five Field Manuals.

    1. The first one can be found after your fist engagement with the Germans. Take them out, fix up the tank, then let it push through the wire. Follow the tank and go right up the hill immediately after you pass the wire.

    You'll see the crate with the Field Manual at the top of the hill.

     

    2. When you see the ruins, drop down into the trench and you'll find yourself in a basement. Walk all the way to the back left and you'll see a light over a gas tank. Take another left into the area with the beds. You'll see the crate with the Field Manual between two beds.

       

    3. After you have access to the bridge, run through the barricade and go under the bridge. Walk to the far side and you'll see the crate with the Field Manual directly under the bridge on a hill.

     

    4. Once you reach the valley with all the anti-tank gear, run up the left path into the Pillbox. You'll find the Field Manual in there.

     

    5. The final Field Manual in the chapter is at the top of the hill past the valley. Clear out the enemies then walk up the hill and take a right down the dirt road. Walk to the end of the camp on the right and you'll see the crate with the Field Manual behind a dirt mound and next to some light wood crates.

     



    EditBreakdown

    There are five Field Manuals in this chapter.

    1. Run up to the first bundle of buildings on the right side of the road. Clear out the enemies then walk to the back left corner of the town and you'll see a door to a small room on the last left building. Kick open the door to reveal the crate with the collectible.

     

    2. Head over to the second windmill in the level and climb up to the last platform before you reach the roof. You'll see the crate with the Field Manual below the window.

     

    3. The third Field Manual is in the bigger, open camp with the fire and tank part not too far from the second windmill. Walk over there, then enter the giant house at the back of the camp. Go up to the attic and you'll find the crate with the Field Manual in a corner next to a box that's draped with a sheet.

    4. The next Field Manual is in the white house next to the alarm. It has a giant flag on it, and is on the street across from the big house where you got the last collectible. Walk in the house and climb up to the top floor. You'll see the crate with the Field Manual on the top floor not too far from the machine gun.

     

    5. The final collectible is in the very back of the map. Go past the camp that has the last engine piece. You'll walk up the little hill then walk to the small shed-like building in the back left corner. You'll find the crate with the Field Manual in there by the door.

       


    EditSteel on Steel

    There are five collectibles in this chapter, and they're very easy. While you drive through the village stop and every windmill, and you'll find each of the Field Manuals. Below are all five windmills. You'll find the crate with the Field Manual behind a beam under the stairs on the first floor in each of the windmills.

       

    EditFriends in High Places

    There are only six Field Manuals in this story.

    EditFall From Grace

    This chapter has five Field Manuals. 

    1. After you kill you first guy with the shovel, walk around the house to the right and follow the dirt road. You'll go right and see a wagon. The crate with the Field Manual is to the left of the wagon.

     

    2. Run down to the first little cluster of buildings you see and walk toward its right side. You'll see a stable-like building that has a closed door. Open the door and you'll find the chest with the Field Manual. 

     

    3. Head over to the next town and enter the tented area on the right of the town. You'll see a few cots. The crate with the Field Manual is between two of the cots.

      

    4. Enter the trenches and follow the path on the right, taking a right at every chance you have. Eventually you'll come across a pillbox up on a hill. Enter the building and you'll see the crate with the Field Manual along the wall of the room.

     

    5. Climb up to the big, muddy clearing in the trenches and look for the dip with the light and overturned cart toward the front center of the clearing. You'll see the crate next to the light.

     

    EditForte et fidele

    There is only one Field Manual to find in this chapter. You can get it after you crash land into the blimp. Walk through the burning interior and you'll end up against two guys just before you get to the gun. Kill them, walk up to the bridge they were on and take a right around the second silver canister. You'll see the crate with the Field Manual in the corner of the little square platform.

     

    EditAvanti Savoia!

    There are 10 totally Field Manuals in this story.

    EditO la Vittoria

    This chapter has five Field Manuals.

    1. After you defeat the enemies at the church, run into the first house you see. A crate with the Field Manual is on the left side of the building next to a covered couch. It's dark in the room, but you'll see the Field Manual icon pop up as you approach the couch.

     

    2. Follow the road after you leave the church and you'll see two baracades. Keep going, and you'll find a trench camp on the left side of the hill. (Climb higher up on the hill if you don't see it.) Fight through the enemies and you'll find the Field Manual between a few crates in the biggest part of the trench. 

     

    3. Go back to the baracaded part in the road and take the path that leads up the hill. You'll eventually find a small, single trench on the left. The crate with the Field Manual is at the end of the trench.

     

    4. Walk down to the artillery gun's bunker. The crate with the Field Manual will be in a corner on your right as you enter the room with the gun's controls.

    5. After destroying the gun follow your men out of the area. You'll go up a hill, and eventually you'll see a covered trench to the right of the path. Clear out the enemies and walk to the entrance of the trench. You'll see the crate with the Field Manual right next to that entrance.

     

    EditO Tutti Accoppati

    There are five Field Manuals in this chapter.

    1. After you spawn, run down the hill toward the south corner of the map. You'll see a stone house. Clear out the enemies inside, then you can grab the Field Manual from the crate near the doors.

     

    2. Once you're free to explore more of the valley, run to the point on the left. You'll find Matteo's unit in the bunker, along with the Field Manual crate to the right of the door you enter.

     

    You'll need to find the following two Field Manuals before entering the fort. You'll be locked out of these areas after you enter the fort courtyard battle. 3. Take the path to the right of the fort. You're going to encounter a lot of enemies on this side. Climb the path up the hill so that you move past the fort and you'll find a buncker lined with a blue doorframe. Enter, and you'll find the Field Manual behind the wall by the tanks.

       

    4. Enter either of the underground entrances to the fort on the left or center path, and make you way to the room with all the beds. You'll find the crate with the Field Manual between two of the bunkbeds by the corner.

     

    5. The final collectible in this story is in the fort. After the enemies are clear, enter the door to the right of the blue doors and exit through the other side. Take a left once outside, and you'll see the crate with the Field Manual by some barrels.

     

    EditThe Runner

    Editcape Helles

    This chapter has five Field Manuals. 

    1. Run to the left along the beach after you reach it. After a bit of running you'll see a few crates against the hill along with the Field Manual icon.

    2. Then, run up to the top of the hill trenches and run along it back toward the boat so that you're in the far north corner of the map. You'll find the crate with the Field Manual in this corner.

     

    3. Then, run back down the trenches toward the flag and covered trench area with the field gun. Walk around to its right side and you'll see the crate with the Field Manual hiding between two walls.

     

    4. Head up the hill after you've cleared the trenches and you'll see a fairly put together house at the top of the hill. Walk up to it and you'll see the crate with the Field Manual in the small closet room.

     

    5. When you're capturing Helles Overlook enter the little stone shed. The Crate with the Field Manual is in the back of the shed.

     

    EditThe Runner

    There are five Field Manuals in this chapter.

    1. When you load in run to the right side of the city. You'll see one house by itself overlooking the cliff. Run in and go to the back left corner of the house. You'll see a door, a bench, and the crate with the Field Manual.

     

    2. Run to the center of the town and you'll see a smoking, overturned cart and an open grassy area. Walk toward the building in the back of that grassy area and you'll see the crate with the Field Manual.

     

    3. Go far left of the city and you'll see a nice house out on its own. Go in, and you'll find a crate with the Field Manual in the back right room.

     

    4. The fourth Field Manual is in the building with the frontline. Get the report, then go to the center room. You'll see the crate with the Field Manual by the main entrance. 

    5. After you get the orders to inform rear command, run toward the objective but walk toward the first house you see overlooking the cliff. It's roof is partially torn off. You'll find the crate with the Field Manual by the front door.

     

    EditBe  Safe

    This chapter have five Field Manuals.

    1. When you load in run toward the center of town toward the crubling stone tower. Climb up to the second floor and you'll see the crate with the Field Manual.

     

    2. Once you're in the fort, climb up to the fort's wall and walk all the way to the left end. You'll see the crate with the Field Manual behind some of the last green crates. 

     

    3. Run to the right end of the wall and you'll see the frames of two destroyed buildings. In the left frame you'll see the crate with the Field Manual.

     

    4. Now, go to the rubble building next to the one where you found the other Field Manual. Look to the back door, and you'll see another open door just past it. Enter, and you'll find a lavish room. The fourth Field Manual is in there.

     

    5. The final Field Manual in the chapter is in the courtyard you capture at the end. In that courtyard you'll see a stone shed. Enter, and you'll see the crate with the Field Manual in the back of the shed.

     

    EditNothing is Written

    EditHidden in Plain Sight

    1. From the beginning of the mission, turn left roughly 90 degrees, then head for the small mountain ahead of you. Tucked amidst the rocks will be the Field Manual.

     

    2. From manual 1, turn around and face the train. Start walking towards it, while keeping your eye out for an outcropping of rocks. Tucked beneath those rocks is the next Field Manual.

     

    3. The next manual is inside the train. Head to the red train car that is slightly South of the objective car. Go inside to find the Field Manual.

     

    4. This manual is right behind number 3. Head outside and walk south-east just a short ways. Tucked behind a rock is the next manual.

    5. The last one requires some walking. From number 4, walk in a south-east direction into the desert. You should soon spot a tiny tent and a tank. Among the rocks near this tent will be the last Field Manual.

     

    EditYoung Men's Work

    1. From the spawning point, loop around behind the mountain. There will be a dugout on the opposite side where you can find the manual.

     

    2. From manual 1, head West. Don't go into the ruins, but just North of them there is a small mountain. Climb up top to find a tent, a few boxes and the manual.

     

    3. The third manual is at the Weapons Depot to the far North of the map. Inside the main building, you'll find the box containing the Field Manual.

     

    4. In the main village in the South of the map, there is a building near the back, with an arched entry. Inside is the manual.

     

    5. On the East side of the village, there is a water tower. Climb it to find the last manual.

     

    EditHear The Desert

    1. From the start, turn right 90 degrees and you will see two small buildings in the distance. Head to them and inside the left one is the Field Manual.

     

    2. Directly after manual 1, exit the building and head West. There are two rocks ahead of you, and leaning against the big rock is the next manual.

     

    3. On the far West side of the village is a water tower. Just past it are some rocks with the manual on it.

     

    4. In the back of the village is a large, 2-story building. Inside is the manual, in the same room as the stairs leading to the next floor.

     

    5. Near the windmill in the southeast of town is a small, semi destroyed building. Inside will be the last of the Field Manuals.
