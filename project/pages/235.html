      Share.

      By Brian Albert

      With the holidays approaching soon, it's time to start thinking about what to get for the Xbox gamer in your life. Tons of great games, console bundles, and controllers are available, so we're here to cut through the fat and show you the best of the best. These are the 22 essential Xbox gifts of the year.

      PDP Energizer 2X White Charging System

      Never buy AA batteries for your Xbox One controllers again! The PDP Energizer 2X Charging System charges two controllers at once, which is perfect for extended co-op or multiplayer sessions. It comes with two battery packs, and it’s also available in white, so it’ll look great next to your shiny, new Xbox One S.

      Gears of War 4

      In our 9.2 Gears of War 4 review, we liken it to The Force Awakens of video games. It copies so much of what made previous Gears games great, but it does it so effectively that we don’t mind that old familiar feeling. With a strong campaign and fully-featured multiplayer and Horde modes, Gears of War 4 is a must have for Xbox shooter fans.

      See Gears of War 4 on Amazon

      See Gears of War 4 on Amazon UK

      Halo 5: Guardians - Limited Edition

      One of the best games to show off your Xbox’s graphical power, Halo 5: Guardians has some of the most fun levels in a Halo game to date, and the best multiplayer since Halo 2. Plus, the Limited Edition comes with a ton of REQ packs to get you in the game faster. Read our Halo 5 review.

      See Halo 5: Guardians - Limited Edition on Amazon

      See Halo 5: Guardians - Limited Edition on Amazon UK

      Destiny The Collection

      There has never been a better time to jump into Destiny, especially if you only played it when it launched. With every major expansion included, you’ll have months of content to play through as you grind for the hottest weapons and flashiest gear. Beware: once this game gets its claws in you, it’s already too late.

      See Destiny The Collection on Amazon

      See Destiny The Collection on Amazon UK

      XCOM 2

      A perfect gift for the person who has a twenty-five-point plan for surviving an alien invasion within their own home.

      See XCOM 2 on Amazon

      See XCOM 2 on Amazon UK

      The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt Complete Edition

      If you’re a fantasy fan and you have a ton of holiday vacation time to burn through, The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt should be your game of choice. In our 9.3 review, we praise the game as “massive in size and meticulously detailed.” This version brings you the base version of the game, plus all the content released to date. Some of it, like Blood and Wine, is very, very good.

      See The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt Complete Edition on Amazon

      See The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt Complete Edition on Amazon UK

      Metal Gear Solid V: The Definitive Experience

      Big Boss in a beautiful, stealthy sandbox, plus a rare 10/10 review from us. What more do you need to hear?

      See Metal Gear Solid V: The Definitive Experience on Amazon

      See Metal Gear Solid V: The Definitive Experience on Amazon UK

      The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim - Special Edition

      Somehow it’s already been five years since Skyrim was released. Even if you played it at launch, that’s enough time to warrant a replay -- especially when it looks this good. Skyrim: Special Edition includes the core game, all of the add-ons, and a ton of new visual effects that will make you forget the game is from 2011.

      See The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim - Special Edition on Amazon

      See The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim - Special Edition on Amazon UK

      Rise of the Tomb Raider

      One of the best action-adventure games on Xbox One, Rise of the Tomb Raider has a great story and tons of devious tombs to keep you busy. Read our Rise of the Tomb Raider review.

      See Rise of the Tomb Raider on Amazon

      See Rise of the Tomb Raider on Amazon UK

      Forza Horizon 3

      Forza Horizon 3 may take place in The Land Down Under, but it’s on top of its game in nearly every way. In our 9.5 review, we called the game “a masterclass in open-world racing and bigger and better than its excellent predecessor across the board.” Gearheads, go nuts.

      See Forza Horizon 3 on Amazon

      See Forza Horizon 3 on Amazon UK

      HGST Touro S 1TB 7200RPM High-Performance Portable Drive

      With an impressive library of great games already out, plus several more coming soon, Xbox One owners might find their original 500 GB hard drives are getting too full. Upgrading to a full terabyte of storage means installing and downloading games won’t be an issue, and deleting old games to make room for the new ones will become a thing of the past. The HGST Touro S 1TB Drive also doesn’t require power, meaning you won’t need to plug in an AC cord. Win!

      The gift that keeps on giving. With an Xbox Live 12 Month Gold Membership, your gamer can play online multiplayer games until next Holiday. Plus, with Microsoft’s free “Games with Gold” program, your lucky recipient will receive free games to download every month. Talk about a deal.

      See Xbox Live 12 Month Gold Membership Card on Amazon

It feels great to hold, the buttons illuminate when you move it, and it’s not overcrowded like most remotes.
