      Share.
      Is Daredevil becoming its own franchise?
      By Jesse Schedeen

      Marvel released their full solicitations for February 2017 today. Look for several new Marvel NOW titles to debut that month, including Kingpin, Elektra and Bullseye, while the X-Men and Inhumans franchises gear up for the conclusion of IvX in March.

      New Series:

      • Bullseye #1
      • Deadpool: Bad Blood HC (original graphic novel)
      • Elektra #1
      • Kingpin #1
      • Star Wars: Darth Maul #1

      Ending Series:

      • The Clone Conspiracy #5
      • Dark Tower: The Drawing of the Three - The Sailor #5
      • Enchanted Tiki Room #5
      • Marvel's Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 Prelude #2

      Observations:

      • We're not sure if the Old Man Logan #18 solicit is supposed to indicate that the series is ending or just the specific story arc. But even if the book is ending, we have to assume a relaunch is being planned alongside the Logan movie in March.
      • Unfortunately, Leinil Yu won't be drawing all of IvX.
      • We're not sure how happy we are to see Elektra's MCU costume make the jump to the comics.
      • If you like the X-Men and Marvel's Epic Collections format, February is shaping up to be an expensive month.
