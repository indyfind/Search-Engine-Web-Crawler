    Bonecharms

    Edit Page    Last Edit: November 19, 2016 - 19 days 16 hours ago

    Bonecharms are an important aspect to giving your character different bonuses. You can find them within levels or craft them using the bonecharm Crafting ability to customize your character with various abilities. When equipped, bonecharms can have four positive traits, but can also a single negative effect if they are corrupted. The more traits a bone charm has, the more likely it is to be corrupted and have a negative effect.

    You can have duplicates of the same ability on a single bonecharm up to four times. You can discover new traits for bonecharms each time you break a regular charm, which can be added to another through crafting. Crafting bone charms makes them more likely to be corrupted, but the negative effects they have can be canceled out with the right abilities.

    EditBonecharm Locations

    • Edge of the World
    • The Good Doctor
    • The Clockwork Mansion
    • The Royal Conservatory
    • The Dust District
    • Crack in the Slab
    • The Grand Palace
    • Death to the Empress

    EditA Long Day in Dunwall

    There are no bonecharms to find in this mission, so you don;t have to worry about missing anything.

    EditEdge of the World

    There is a total of six bonecharms to find on this mission.

    EditBonecharm #1

    To the right of the Wall of Light is an apartment before the canal area that's being raided by Overseers. A bonecharm is on the floor of the second story building.

    EditBonecharm #2

    The infested building behind the Black Market has a bonecharm on the second floor, can be picked up once you destroy the nest inside.

    EditBonecharm #3

    You can Blink/Far Reach across rooftops from the Overseer building to a roof with a dead body on it. The bonecharm is on the corpse near the Bloodflies.

    EditBonecharm #4

    Outside the Overseer building is a bonecharm next to a Rune. The civilians listening to the Overseers outside won't care if you steal the stuff, so Blink or Far Reach behind the Overseers and slowly creep up onto the tables behind them to take the goods.

    EditBonecharm #5

    Near the top of the Overseer building is a locked Interrogation Room. The key to the room is in the room to the right with an Overseer nearby (as well as an open window). The bonecharm found inside is corrupted.

    EditBonecharm #6

    You can find this bonecharm in the Winslow Safe Company giant safe on the main floor. The combination to open the safe is random each playthrough, and can be found in a note in the cash register by the front.

    EditThe Good Doctor

    You can find five bonecharms while completing the missions.

    EditBonecharm #1

    At the start of the mission, look for a pipe behind you underneath the walkway. If you Blink/Far Reach downward, there is a bonecharm on the platform.

    EditBonecharm #2

    As soon as you enter the Atrium of the Addermire Institute, look for an abandoned cart of luggage in the center of the room that hides the Bonecharm.

    EditBonecharm #3

    A black bonecharm is on the highest roof of the area. By going to the fourth floor and heading outside on the balcony, climb up above the private rooms and Blink/Far Reach to the middle of the roof, you will find a bird's nest with the black bonecharm past a smokestack.

    EditBonecharm #4

    In the Recuperation Room on the third floor, open the tanning booths on the left side of the large room and go through the one that allows you to crawl through into a damaged room with a bonecharm.

    EditBonecharm #5

    A corrupted bonecharm is located in a small office near Dr. Hypatia's lab at the bottom of the Recuperation Room.




    EditThe Clockwork Mansion

    You will find eight bonecharms here in various parts of the mission.

    EditBonecharm #1

    Near the main street of the district as you head up the stairs, Blink/Far Reach to a short wall of a building and then jump to a balcony over the road. Go into the office and break the china cabinet to find a bonecharm.

    EditBonecharm #2

    Near where civilians are located by the Aventa Station, Blink/Far Reach up some pipes and then to a balcony that is above the area. Climb into an apartment and you will find a black bonecharm in a display case.

    EditBonecharm #3

    A bonecharm can be found on the second floor of the carriage station, on some crates near the carriage itself.

    EditBonecharm #4

    In the Upper Aventa District, a bonecharm is located inside a guard building near the carriage, on the second floor inside the Records Room. Wait until the moving guard examines some shelves before taking it

    EditBonecharm #5

    Also in the Upper Aventa District, past the Wall of Light there is a Bloodfly-infested building. Hop up the awning and into the second floor window, where you'll find the bonecharm on a stool.

    EditBonecharm #6

    Find Jindosh's prviate bedroom near his laboratory, and look in the main bedroom for a small closet behind a workshop. Inside the closet use a button to lower the closet down to the bonecharm in some shelves.

    EditBonecharm #7

    On the Assessment Chamber level, pull a lever that brings up the guest waiting area. Rush into an area between the two floors and follow the path between the floors for a crawlspace. Further up you'll find a body infested with bloodflies who holds the bonecharm.

    EditBonecharm #8

    Past the Assessment Chamber is a small Wall of Light blocking access to Jindosh's laboratory. Look in the drawers in the room with the Wall of Light for the bonecharm.



    EditThe Royal Conservatory

    There are nine bonecharms to find in this mission.

    EditBonecharm #1

    After the first Wall of Light, look for a large red building that has a pub on the first floor. A bonecharm lies on the card-playing table.

    EditBonecharm #2

    From the red building with the pub, jump off the rooftop to a balcony on the building opposite the pub. Inside the bloodfly infested Lecture Hall is a Nest Keeper by a locked safe. The bonecharm rests next to both of them on the floor.

    EditBonecharm #3

    While undertaking the shopkeeper's associate quest, there is a bonecharm in a building near the Royal Conservatory. The bonecharm is located on the third floor next to a blueprint on a table.

    EditBonecharm #4

    The basement of the Conservatory has a locked door to the Quartermaster's Room. Look left of the door for a small window by the ceiling, and crawl out of it and then back in though another opening further up to slip into the room, and find the Bonecharm inside.

    EditBonecharm #5

    On the third floor of the Conservatory, a bonecharm is floating in front of a chalkboard on the front side of the building, near the Bloodflies nest. You can bypass the flies by warping up from the room below that has two Witches and a body stuffed with sleeping darts.

    EditBonecharm #6

    On the fourth floor of the Conservatory in a storage room above Ashworth's office is a black bonecharm.

    EditBonecharm #7

    There is another bonecharm on the fourth floor. You can reach it by summoning the elevator to the top floor, and crawling up through the hatch in the elevator to find an alcove with the bonecharm in it.

    EditBonecharm #8 and #9

    Breanna Ashworth's body has two black bonecharms you can loot. If you don't want to kill her, use the Oracular Device on her so that she doesn't fight back, and then either perform a drop knockout or sliding knockout to take her down (sleep darts and choking her just make her mad).



    EditThe Dust District

    This mission has eight bonecharms that can be found.

    EditBonecharm #1

    There is a bonecharm inside a locked desk in the back of the black market. You can't buy it, so you must instead rob the place - but the door has a combination lock. Head out the back alley behind the store, and go up the slope until you can see a blocked door through a window above the Black Market, and destroy the boards with a crossbow bolt.

    Enter the room above the Black Market to find a wedding photo in the far corner opposite the sleeping man along with a note. Remember the month, and look at a calendar in the hall and look for a circled date on that month. The month is the first digit, and the date are the other two digits.

    EditBonecharm #2

    Enter the Crone's Hand Pub, and sneak over to the plaza where Paolo and some music players are. Look for an alcove to the right that leads to a basement where an Overseer is being tortured. In this basement, a bonecharm lies on one of the tables.

    EditBonecharm #3, #4, and #5

    You can obtain a total of three bonecharms from Paolo by either pickpocketting or looting him after he is dead. Remember that he must be killed or knocked out twice - the first time he will turn into a swam of rats and respawn back at his shrine up on the third floor.

    EditBonecharm #6

    Head to the Overseer's Outpost and get to Byrne's office on the third floor. The small desk in the corner holds the bonecharm - you'll have to wait until Byrne is looking at his bulletin board if you want to steal it without getting caught.

    EditBonecharm #7

    A bonecharm is located on the second floor of a damaged building in the Overseer territory. You can Blink/Far Reach into a window of the building and break some wooden boards to get inside and grab the bonecharm in the nearby room.

    EditBonecharm #8

    Grab Durante's Key from Byrne's office on the third floor of the Overseer Outpost. Then, return to the Crone's Hand Pub, and find Durante's office on the second floor. The bonecharm is inside once you unlock it.


    EditCrack in the Slab

    There are three (but possibly seven) bonecharms to find in this mission.

    EditBonecharm #1

    This bonecharm is located inside a safe in the boiler room. In order to get to it, enter the Vault behind the Dining Room in the present, then warp to the past and knock down the statue before returning to the present and crawling through to a path to the Boiler Room (warp under the desk at the far end to come out on the other side of a barricade.

    Since the safe's combination can't be read in the present, warp to the past and incinerate the wolfhound's corpse in the boiler, then return to the present to read the now legible safe door combination.

    EditBonecharm #2

    Enter the basement of the mansion in the present, and look for an elevator in the corner. Jump onto a small lip above the elevator entrance and transition into the past and present to get through a hatch into the elevator. The bonecharm is found inside.

    EditBonecharm #3

    Use the stairs to go to the second floor of the mansion. Get onto the balcony that is over the dining room, and warp to the past, noting that the chandeliers aren't aligned in both times. Look for lever controls in the corner of the balcony and pull the farthest one to align it with the chandelier in the present, then jump across all three before warping to the present. From here you can climb up the ruined ceiling to the attic where a bonecharm sits by a corpse.

    EditBonecharm #4

    Note that his bonecharm can ONLY be found if you decided to knock Aramis Stilton unconcious in the past, therby changing his fate as well as the fate of the mansion itself. In the past, there is a piece of wall by the second floor elevator that notes a planned office room to be built. If Stilton is alive and well, the office will be built in the new version of the present, and the bonecharm will be on his desk.

    EditBonecharm #5

    Like the previous Bonecharm, this one can ONLY be found if you knock Stilton unconcious in the past. After leaving the mansion behind and returning to the Dust District, the place will have changed and the apartment below where you met Meagan Foster will now be the home of Lucia Pastor. In a backroom of this updated home, a bonecharm lies by an open window.

    EditBonecharm #6

    Like the previous Bonecharm, this one can ONLY be found if you knock Stilton unconcious in the past. Returning to the Dust District, there will no longer be a Wall of Light or guards - but there will be a Bonecharm up on the roof in some shelves near where the windmill generator used to be.

    EditBonecharm #7

    Like the previous Bonecharm, this one can ONLY be found in you knock Stilton unconcious in the past. When you return to the sewers where Sokolv is waiting, look to the left where there used to be health potion against the wall, and there will be be bonecharm now.




    EditThe Grand Palace

    There are seven bonecharms to find in this mission.

    EditBonecharm #1

    A black bonecharm is near the balcony across from the Winslow Safe Company store. Warp up to the second floor to find a sculptor's studio and open a vice against the wall where you can find the black bonecharm.

    EditBonecharm #2

    Near the Ravina district's Wall of Light on the left are two apartment buildings. The first has a bonecharm in the bathroom on the third floor in Lucia Pastor's old apartment.

    EditBonecharm #3

    Search the apartment closest to the Wall of Light. You can unlock the door to the third floor apartment with a Key from Winslow Safe Company's store cashier, where the bonecharm is on a bed under a pillow.

    EditBonecharm #4

    In the Grand Palace itself, check the throne room for a bonecharm on the throne itself. However note that it is a trap as an Arc Pylon is stationed nearby, meaning you will have to duck around the back to take out the whale oil tank powering it.

    EditBonecharm #5

    There is a bonecharm in a small room on the second floor of the palace to the right of the Dining Hall. Go left from the stairs to find a small room with two beds and two women. The bonecharm is on a bed in the small suite - so you may have to distract or knock out the women so they don't run for help.

    EditBonecharm #6

    In the Palace, head down the main stairs to the bottom floor and enter the pantry. Search the shelves to the left of a large wall for a button below some bottles that opens up the Duke's Safe Room and Vault. A bonecharm can be found in front of a shrine to Delilah off to the side of the briefing room.

    EditBonecharm #7

    You can find a corrupted bonecharm on the fourth floor of the palace in Delilah's Room, inside one of the bathrooms.


    EditDeath to the Empress

    There are a total of nine bonecharms to find in this mission before the end of the game.

    EditBonecharm #1 and #2

    Not technically located in Dunwall, you can get two Bonecharms aboard the Dreadful Wale. Pickpocket or kill Meagan Foster to get the key to her private room next to the briefing room, and you'll find the bonecharms inside.

    EditBonecharm #3

    Before entering Dunwall Tower from the streets, climb back up the rooftops on the right - heading back to the abandoned corridor leading to the Safe Room you escaped from back in Mission 1. The way is blocked, but a side room has a bonecharm lodged up near the ceiling.

    EditBonecharm #4

    On the path to Dunwall Tower is a Greenhouse located in a building to the right of a large wooden fence. A bonecharm can be found within a basket inside - which you can sneak to while the Witches inside are harvesting resources and chanting.

    EditBonecharm #5

    Further beyond the Greenhouse is a small bridge leading to the Waterlock boathouse you may remember from the start of Dishonored 1. You can find a bonecharm around the back of the building on a balcony guarded by two Witches. To get to the balcony, head towards the gazebo and a painting, and look along the ramparts for large pipes moving alongside the building to the back.

    EditBonecharm #6 and #7

    You can obtain two bonecharms from the High Overseer at the main entrance to Dunwall Tower, where he is strung up on a post.

    EditBonecharm #8

    On the third floor of Dunwall Tower, head up the main stairs in the middle, and look to the right for a path to a hall guarded by a Clockwork Soldier. Just inside the doorway are two openings up by the ceiling. Warp and climb into the room on the left to find a holed up Civilian with the bonecharm nearby.

    EditBonecharm #9

    Opposite the previous bonecharm, head to the other large hall and look for the same openings near the ceiling. The room on the left has a Witch guarding the Bonecharm - sneak in from above and take her out before stealing it.
