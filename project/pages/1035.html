    Pilot Helmet Locations

    Edit Page    Last Edit: November 4, 2016 - 1 month 4 days ago

    Pilot Helmets are the Titanfall 2 collectibles. This is a complete guide to every Pilot Helmet location in the Titanfall 2 campaign. For a more contextual look at where each collectible is in every level, be sure to check out the Walkthrough.

    There are 46 Pilot Helmets hidden throughout Titanfall 2's campaign. Here is a video showing the location of all 46 followed by a text guide with accompanying pictures.

    All 46 Pilot Helmet Locations - Titanfall 2 Walkthrough
    16:08
    Got feedback on our player?
    We want to hear it.

    The Pilot's Gauntlet (1) BT-7274 (2) Blood and Rust (6)
    Into the Abyss (9) Effect and Cause (8) The Beacon (11)
    Trial By Fire (3) The Ark (3) The Fold Weapon (3)

    EditThe Pilot’s Gauntlet

    There's only one Pilot Helmet in this mission.

    All The Pilot's Gauntlet Pilot Helmet Locations - Titanfall 2 Walkthrough
    00:24
    Got feedback on our player?
    We want to hear it.

    1. You’ll find the Pilot Helmet while you’re running through the Pilot Evaluation. Just before you reach the finish line, jump left to the floating pillars. To the left of those pillars are two more that are slightly higher. You’ll see the Pilot Helmet on the shorter of the two. Double jump over to them and you can collect the helmet.

     

    EditBT-7274

    There are two Pilot Helmets in this mission.

    All BT-7274 Pilot Helmet Locations - Titanfall 2 Walkthrough
    00:34
    Got feedback on our player?
    We want to hear it.

    1. You’ll find this Pilot Helmet easily while you follow the waypoint for BT’s replacement battery. Shortly after leaving BT you’ll see an empty escape pod and two creatures. The helmet is next to the escape pod.

    2. After you grab the third battery for BT, exit the ship. One you’re back outside you’ll see the second Pilot Helmet on rubble hanging off a cliff. Run high along the wall on the right and double jump to get to the helmet.

     

    EditBlood and Rust

    There are six Pilot Helmets in Blood and Rust.

    All Blood and Rust Pilot Helmet Locations - Titanfall 2 Walkthrough
    01:44
    Got feedback on our player?
    We want to hear it.

    1. You’ll see the first Pilot Helmet in this level just after BT picks up his new weapon. The helmet is behind a gate, but you can get it by walking under and just past the pipes that lead into the gated area. Then, turn around and look up at the red lights where the pipes go past the gate. Exit BT close to the pipes (you must be on the far side of the pipes) and jump as soon as you’re out. You should be able to grab onto the pipe and enter the gated area to grab the Pilot Helmet.

       

    2. After you have to leave BT, climb up the stairs and take out any enemies. Then go up to the platform, run along the wall and climb up to the double pipes above the red light. Turn around back toward the entrance. The Pilot Helmet you’re looking for is on top of the second huge cylindrical container. Run along the wall and jump to it.

     

    3. Once you climb through the pipes to first find the friendly forces in the facility, look toward the left before jumping over to them. You’ll see the helmet up on another platform. Jump over to the pipe on the left, and use your wall run and double jump to get up to the helmet.

     

    4.This next one is a little tricky to get and could take a few tries if you don’t get the jump right. After helping out your surviving allies, you’ll jump over the corrosive river and end up on the side where the enemies were shooting at you behind a few trucks. Go through the big doors, and immediately look to the left. You’ll see a chemical pool and broken pipes with the helmet in the very back. Run along the left side of this little room till you’re at the shore of the chemical pool. Then, run along the wall and jump up to the pipes just as you start to drop. You should catch the pipe and be able to get the Pilot Helmet.

       

    5. Eventually you’ll come to a tall room where two flying robots are making repairs, and there are some large yellow pipes on the left side of the room. This is Inlet 13, and you’ll find your next Pilot Helmet here. Look up, and you’ll see the helmet high up on a bridge. You know what to do. Climb up the pipes on the left and then jump on the next smaller yellow pipes. Run along them, jump and run along the wall once the pipes turn, and you should be able to grab onto the bigger grey pipe in the center then bounce off to get to the bridge with the Pilot Helmet.

       

    6. The final Pilot Helmet is in the room you enter after getting the previous helmet. Look up at the exit to this tall room, and you’ll see the Pilot Helmet on the platform next to the way out. Climb all the way up, but instead of landing next to the working robot, use your double jump to jump over the robot and onto the sludge waterfall platform with the helmet.

     

    EditInto the Abyss

    The Into the Abyss Pilot Helmet locations are broken up into three parts. In the first part you'll find four helmets.

    All Into the Abyss Pilot Helmet Locations - Titanfall 2 Walkthrough
    04:29
    Got feedback on our player?
    We want to hear it.

    1. After you drop down into the first big open area, go right and you’ll see a little cave on its own. It's near the big opening that lets natural light in. Jump out of BT and you’ll find the first Pilot Helmet there.

     

    2. In the same area, walk to the left side and you’ll see a huge crack in the rock that leads to another big area. Jump out of BT to get through the crack, and jump onto the platform just across the gap. You’ll see a smoking escape pod. You’ll then need to run to the right to the other ledge, and you’ll find the Pilot Helmet on that platform’s ledge overlooking the water.

     

    3. Once you’ve entered the room with the trees, couches, and cars being carted around on the hanging tracks, clear out the enemies and walk all the way to the back cliff wall where you turn the corner to move on to the next area. Instead of going down that hall, though, jump out of BT and onto the stone wall and up onto the pipes. Follow them along the cliff and you’ll reach the Pilot Helmet. Be careful not to fall!

       

    4. The next Pilot Helmet is under the landing pad. Jump out of BT, and instead of going to call the cargo lift, climb down on the rocks below the landing pad. Look down to the landing pad’s pillars and you’ll see the helmet. Climb down to it on either side of the landing pad to grab it. There are lots of ledges to jump on, so platforming down to it should be fairly easy.

       

    EditInto the Abyss Part 2

    For some reason the nine collectibles in Into the Abyss are split into three sections. Here’s where you’ll find the next three after BT is carried away by the crane.

    1. The first Pilot Helmet in this section is in the pipe room just after you get split up with BT. You’ll spot it pretty easily across a huge gap. To get to it you’ll want to get a running double jump across to the pillar in the middle, run along it, then jump over to the platform with the helmet.

     

    2. Once you’re on the platform where the pallet is mostly built -- it has a frame, furniture, and grass -- you’ll jump off onto a rather large section that has a second floor. Climb up to the second floor or take the lift. The little enclosure where the lift lands is where you’ll find the Pilot Helmet. Look up, and you’ll see a set of pipes running along the ceiling. Use the short pile of crates to the right of the small hallway that lead to nowhere to get a little boost, wallrun for a very short time, and double jump up to the pipes. Walk along the pipes and you’ll be able to get to the Pilot Helmet.

       

    3. After you climb your way out of the starting sideways house, wallrun to the left and land on the other sideways building. Peek over the side of the building and you’ll see the Pilot Helmet by a door on the rock wall. Jump across to the bridge then run along the rock wall to get the Pilot Helmet.

     

    EditInto the Abyss Part 3

    After you’ve escaped the dome you enter the final part of this mission. There are two Pilot Helmets to find in this area.

    1. Exit the rocky tunnel and you’ll find yourself in a huge room with three different columns. Look up to column 83 and you’ll see the Pilot Helmet floating. Hit the green button to push out a bunch of containment pods. Use these to climb up to the Pilot Helmet.

     

    2. Just before you meet up with BT you’ll have to cross a chasm by running along a moving wall. Do that, but instead of jumping into the arena, walk along the edge of the outside and you should see the Pilot Helmet. Climb the rocks along the edge of the dome and you’ll be able to get the helmet.

     

    EditEffect and Cause

    There are two Pilot Helmets in the first part of the mission, and they’re both in the same area.

    All Effect and Cause Pilot Helmet Locations - Titanfall 2 Walkthrough
    02:34
    Got feedback on our player?
    We want to hear it.

    1. Once you find BT in the building plaza, look down to building 02 (it’s marked with the 02) while you’re still at the window. You should see the Pilot Helmet floating along the side of the building. Climb up to building 02’s roof and drop down along the side to collect the helmet.

    2. The next Pilot Helmet is next to BT on the top of a light poll. Climb to the roof of the next building and double jump over to the light pole to get the helmet.

     

    EditEffect and Cause Part 2

    After you make it to Anderson’s other half you’ll get a fresh assignment to collect six more Pilot Helmets.

    1. In the fancy facility (past), you’ll find the first helmet in the elevator shaft. You should be able to get it while you’re climbing up the walls, or you can jump out to the Pilot Helmet once you get to a high enough platform.

     

    2. You’ll come to a hallway in the present that has a fiery floor, but a Pilot Helmet floating at the end in the air. Wall run to it. If you miss, just keep wall running till you pick it up.

     

    3. Before you get to the cryo storage facility you’ll be greeted by three turrets in the past. Go back to the present and jump into the hanging cylindrical platform. Flip back to the past and you can get the Pilot Helmet that’s on the platform.

     

    4. When you reach the giant, open cryo storage room you’ll see the Pilot Helmet on a platform not too far away. Go to the past to jump on the first platform, then flip to the present to land on the platform with the Pilot Helmet.

     

    5.You’ll see this Pilot Helmet toward the exit of the giant cryo storage room. It’s high up between two walls where you have to flip back and forth between the past and present to get across. Keep jumping back and forth till you can it. If you happen to die getting it, that’s okay too! You’ll still collect it even if you fall.

    6.The final collectible is in the present just past the wall you have to jump across by flipping back and forth through time. Make sure you’re in the present then run along the window and jump to the Pilot Helmet. If you have the bridge open you should land on it after you jump to get the helmet.

     

    EditThe Beacon

    This mission is split up into three parts, but the third part is just a return to the first area and doesn't reset your Pilot Helmet count. There are a total of 11 Pilot Helmets across the two. You can start the mission by grabbing two the following two helmets, or you can get them after you emerge from the substation.

    All The Beacon Pilot Helmet Locations - Titanfall 2 Walkthrough
    03:26
    Got feedback on our player?
    We want to hear it.

    1. After you save the stranded soldiers, don’t go into the facility with them just yet. Walk over to the satellite facility with BT, and climb up to the platform below the dish. You’ll find a Pilot Helmet on the back right side of the platform next to a pillar. You can also get this after you come out of the substation.

     

    2. While still on the platform look to the back left building that has a cable coming off it. You’ll see another Pilot Helmet. Use the shorter pillars to platform over to the building’s roof and grab the helmet. You can also get this after you exit the substation.

     

    EditThe Beacon Part 2

    The next two Pilot Helmets can only be found in the substation.

    1. This first one is hard to miss. After you clear out the bots in substation 03’s server room, walk across the spinning beam to grab the Pilot Helmet

    2. After you get the arc tool you’ll find yourself on top of another fan. Use the arc tool to turn it on, but instead of jumping out of the room, jump on the ledge on the opposite wall to get the next Pilot Helmet.

     

    EditThe Beacon Part 3

    With the helmets in the substation collected you’ll go back to finding the other nine hidden throughout the facility. If you didn’t collect the first two before entering the substation do that now.

    3. BT throws you, and you’ll land on the first of many work stations. This one, however, has a Pilot Helmet! Get out your arc tool and open the locked door on this station’s first floor. You’ll find the helmet here.

     

    4. Move the first wall with the crane, then jump across to work station 02. The station across the bridge from it has the Pilot Helmet on its roof. Climb up the wall and grab it!

     

    5. The next Pilot Helmet is on the big platform just before the dish with the two cranes. It’s easy to spot since it’s on the roof of the tallest building of the second platform in this area. Climb up to the roof to grab it.

     

    6. When you first jump over to the structure that’s holding up the dish, you’ll meet a new wave of enemies that lock the bridge to the other side. Instead of wall running to the other side once they’re dead, jump up on the ledge above the door to get the Pilot Helmet.

     

    7. The next Pilot Helmet is just above the 04 building that houses the satellite dish’s controls. Run across the work area and climb up to the roof above the 04 to grab the Pilot Helmet.

     

    8. After BT throws you for the second time, walk to the left and you’ll see the Pilot Helmet on the corner of the staircase.

     

    9. The last Pilot Helmet in this mission is in the base of the actual dish. Once you climb up to its support beams, walk around till you see the Pilot Helmet out on one of the beams. Its glow makes it easy to find. Walk out on the curved beam and grab it.

     

    EditTrial by Fire

    There are three Pilot Helmets in this mission.

    All Trial By Fire Pilot Helmet Locations - Titanfall 2 Walkthrough
    00:39
    Got feedback on our player?
    We want to hear it.

    1. Jump out of BT at the first ledge you see on your left after you enter the base with Commander Sarah Briggs. Walk between the two machines and you'll see the Pilot Helmet.

     

    2. Before you get on the lift, follow the yellow cable on the ground that leads to a hole between two shipping containers. Crawl through it and follow the little crawl space to another room. You’ll find the Pilot Helmet here.

     

    3. After the lift, walk up the road a bit and jump out of BT when it makes its first turn to the left. Instead, jump down to the tanks on the right. You'll see the Pilot Helmet on one of the tank's pipes.

     

    EditThe Ark

    There are three Pilot Helmets in this mission.

    All The Ark Pilot Helmet Locations - Titanfall 2 Walkthrough
    00:56
    Got feedback on our player?
    We want to hear it.

    1. After you land on the transporter, run past the enemies (you can deal with them later) straight for the last deck that faces somewhat toward the ship. Out there is the first Pilot Helmet in a small inlet on the ship. Jump across to it. To be completely honest, the easiest way to get out of this area is to jump off and reload at the last checkpoint.

       

    2. This helmet is actually pretty challenging, and you’ll likely fail a few times before you can get it. Once Briggs pull up to the side of the IMC Transport to help your team get to the main deck, you’ve gotta jump onto her ship and run into it before she closes the door. Just behind her is the Pilot Helmet. She’l wait to close the door till you jump back off the ship.

     

    3. After you clear the main deck take the short set of stairs up to the main level of the jet. Don’t go up or down the bigger sets of stairs to the left. Walk all the way to the end row and you’ll see the Pilot Helmet next to the last aircraft.

     

    EditThe Fold Weapon

    There are three Pilot Helmets in this final mission.

    All The Fold Weapon Pilot Helmet Locations - Titanfall 2 Walkthrough
    01:39
    Got feedback on our player?
    We want to hear it.

    1. Once you reach the long outside power grid area with the turrets positioned in the windows, you’re going to need to climb up to the roof of the A30 building, then to get to the top of the TMD Power Grid 10 unit. On the top of this unit on the left you’ll see the Pilot Helmet.

     

    2. BT and Cooper will loose the Militia’s assistance once you make your way inside the facility. In this first big circular room you should see the Pilot Helmet directly in the center back on a pipe. Run to the back left corner and climb on the storage crates. Use those to wall run up to the second ledge on the back wall. Now you can walk over to the Pilot Helmet.

       

    3. You’ll find the last Pilot Helmet in the blue schematics room. Walk to the back right corner of the room, then jump out of BT to climb up to the back wall. You’ll see the Pilot Helmet on the highest pipe.
