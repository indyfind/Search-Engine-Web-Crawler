    About This Game

    • Summary
    • Game Editions

    Minecraft: Story Mode is an all-new narrative-driven game series developed by Telltale Games in collaboration with Mojang. This episodic game series is set in the world of Minecraft, and features an original story which is driven by player choice -- it's like a playable television show based on your favorite game, where YOU are the star, and your choices and actions decide how the story is told. Minecraft: Story Mode features new characters in an entirely original Minecraft experience, inspired by the Minecraft community and the game that continues to inspire a generation.

    You are Jesse (a character -- male or female -- that you create,) and you and your friends are thrown into an awe-inspiring adventure to save the world. While visiting EnderCon, your gang stumbles on something dreadful. Terror is unleashed, and your only hope is to track down the "Order of the Stone", a fabled group of adventurers who slew the legendary Ender Dragon. But where are they? In a race against time, you will travel across the over-world, through the Nether, to The End, and beyond.
