    Upgradable Weapons

    Edit Page    Last Edit: December 9, 2016 - 1 hour 35 minutes ago

    In Final Fantasy XV, Noctis can find a variety of rare but potentially powerful Upgradable Weapons throughout the land of Eos. While mildly powerful on their own, these weapons can be given to Cid the Mechanic, an in exchange for certain Treasures, they can be upgraded to become even more powerful in combat - sometimes several times! Many times, upgrading a weapon may take time to prepare, requiring Noctis to go on a different quest or bounty hunt to pass the time.

    See below for a list of weapons, where to find them, and how they can be upgraded.

(Requires Hunter Rank 7). Both bounty hunts are repeatable.

    • How to Upgrade: Start Cid's quest - Cid Sophiar, Master Mechanic - and find and give Cid a Metal Scrap. They can also be found at Longwythe Rest Area behind the Crow's Diner or along the motel rooms, and even on the main boardwalk of Galdin Quay leading to the resort.

They will also always drop from an Elder Coeurl, which can be hunted in the Taelpar Bounty Hunt - Old Denizens of the Woods.
