      Share.
      SATA Claus is coming to town.
      By Brian Albert

      Ah, PC gamers. This bunch may dazzle you with talk of overclocking and liquid cooling, but the good news is you don't have to understand a word of that to buy them great gifts this holiday season. From figures to games to gear, these are the best gifts you can get for a PC enthusiast this year.

      Overwatch Funko Pops

      From memes to fan fiction to machinima, Overwatch is the game that keeps on giving thanks largely to its unique, lovable cast. For those moments when you have to be AFK, Funko Pop has you covered with miniature versions of Reaper, Winston, Tracer, and several other Overwatch characters. You could even make them fight, or hug. We won’t judge.

      See Overwatch Funko Pops on Amazon

      Doom Cacodemon Plush

      Classic and cute, Doom’s iconic Cacodemon isn’t so dangerous when it’s 5” tall.

      See the Doom Cacodemon Plush on Amazon

      The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt Complete Edition Collector's Guide: Prima Collector's Edition Guide

      Let’s be real. The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt Complete Edition is huge. Like, huge huge. Potentially hundreds of hours huge. And yes, part of the fun is exploring the world and finding out the cool secrets for yourself, but your holiday break is only so long. With the Prima Collector’s Edition Guide, you can see all the best Witcher 3 content in a week or two and still have some time to talk to people in real life. Maybe.

      See The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt Complete Edition Collector's Guide: Prima Collector's Edition Guide on Amazon

      EVGA GeForce GTX 1070 SC GAMING ACX 3.0, 8GB GDDR5 Graphics Card

      Now we’re cooking with gas. The GeForce GTX 1070 is one of the beefiest graphics cards on the market. It has enough pixel-pumping power to run most games at their highest settings with room to spare. If you’re buying this for someone, it’s best to check that their PC will be compatible. Yeah, it can ruin the surprise, but something tells us they won’t mind.

      See EVGA GeForce GTX 1070 SC GAMING ACX 3.0, 8GB GDDR5 Graphics Card on Amazon

      EVGA GeForce GTX 1060 SC GAMING, 6GB GDDR5 Graphics Card

      The GeForce GTX 1060 has plenty of power, and at only 6.8” long, it’s perfect for PC gamers with compact cases.

      See EVGA GeForce GTX 1060 SC GAMING, 6GB GDDR5 Graphics Card on Amazon

      Dell Computer Ultrasharp U2415 24.0-Inch Screen LED Monitor

      This Dell Ultrasharp monitor packs some excellent bang for your buck. At under $250, 24” of 1920x1080 real estate is a great deal, and its color depth will make all of those high-end PC games look even better.

      See Dell Computer Ultrasharp U2415 24.0-Inch Screen LED Monitor on Amazon

      Doom started on PCs, and now, over 20 years later, the newest version is still best enjoyed with a keyboard and mouse. Doom is beautiful, frenzied, and perfect on a lightning-fast gaming rig. Earlier this year, we praised the single-player campaign for replicating what’s great about the original, but for a new generation. Read our Doom review.

Its cast of unique characters, fun levels, and addictive, short matches make it hard to stop playing, and even when you’re away from your PC, you’re left thinking about new character matchups and combinations. Also, there are so many Overwatch memes out there now that you sort of need to play the game to understand the internet.
