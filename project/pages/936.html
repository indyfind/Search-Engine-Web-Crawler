    Resident Evil VII Wiki Guide

    Edit Page    Last Edit: December 3, 2016 - 5 days 6 hours ago
     
    Released Platforms
    January 24, 2017
    Pre-Order
    		 PlayStation 4, PSVR, Xbox One, PC
    Developer Publisher
    Capcom Capcom

    EditDeveloper

    Capcom are the developers behind the Resident Evil franchise. They are also responsible for the Mega Man, Devil May Cry, Monster Hunter, and Dead Rising franchises.

    EditE3 2016

    At E3 2016, it was announced that Resident Evil VII will be releasing on January 24th, 2017 for PlayStation 4, PSVR, Xbox One, and PC.

    Resident Evil 7: "Desolation" Trailer - E3 2016
    03:50
    Got feedback on our player?
    We want to hear it.

    The gameplay trailer that we saw had us walking through a very run down house. It's unclear at this time if the game will have anything to do with the previous games, but at this time it appears that Resident Evil VII is largely a standalone title as there were no callbacks to the previous games.

    EditPlayStation Experience 2016

    During PlayStation Experience 2016, we got a new trailer for Resident Evil VII: Biohazard. It showed us new environments, locations, and enemies.

    Resident Evil 7: Biohazard PSX 2016 Trailer
    01:31
    Got feedback on our player?
    We want to hear it.


    EditCharacters

    No Characters have been confirmed for Resident Evil 7. The people we see in the demo are only found there. We learned this during an interview with the director of the game, where he stated the Demo is separate from the game itself.

    The Full 17 MInute Resident Evil 7 Demo Will Wreck You
    17:12
    Got feedback on our player?
    We want to hear it.

    During the same interview, we were told that it's unlikely we will see classic characters in the game, such as Chris or Jill, but we should expect surprises.

    EditSetting

    As of now, the area that RE7 takes place is unconfirmed. During our interview, we were told it took place in the Resident Evil universe, but that it won't feature zombies or characters we've seen before.

    With a focus on a more personal horror story, it's possible that the game is taking place in a new town. We know there is going to be combat, so it's possible that you will play as a test patient for a new virus, or are trying to escape a monstrous outbreak.

    EditEnemies

    Keeping in tone with this new setting, it appears that there will be far fewer enemies, with an emphasis on enemies being menacing and powerful. Unlike the mindless zombies or monsters from past games, these enemies are to be feared.

    IGN Reacts to Resident Evil 7 VR Hands On - IGN Access
    03:34
    Got feedback on our player?
    We want to hear it.

    What those enemies are remained unknown, and we have yet to find out if they will be humans, monsters, or supernatural creatures.

    EditVR

    Resident Evil 7 will feature full VR compatibility. If you do not have access to VR, or find it to be a bit too much, you can also play the game like a standard First Person game.

    EditDemo

    A free playable Demo is available on the PSN Store for PlayStation Plus subscribers. The demo walks you through a similar area to what was shown off in the E3 trailer, though it is not part of the main game.

    During an interview with the game's director, it was announced that the Resident Evil 7 Demo is a standalone product designed to showcase the new game's design. Since it's not a demo of the game itself, it's possible the final game will feature zombies, monsters, or previous characters from Resident Evil.
