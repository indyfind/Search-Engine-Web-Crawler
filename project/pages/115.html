    Side Quests

    Edit Page    Last Edit: December 9, 2016 - 9 minutes ago

    The world of Final Fantasy XV contains countless Side Quests that span the land of Eos and beyond, as many people in every region can use a helping hand.

    Side Quests can often be found in various towns and outposts - as well as certain locations out in the wilderness. These range from helping people with simple problems, making dangerous deliveries - to scouring the lands for special items and more.

    Certain Side Quests only appear once a certain chapter or story point has been reached - and characters will often have additional follow-up quests later. For more information, consult the Side Quests list below.

    See Also: Bounty Hunts

    EditSide Quests

    The following side quests are organized by the region in which the quest starts, and the recommended level they should be undertaken. Note that if you do not see a quest in the indicated area, it may only be available during a later chapter, or part of a series of quests from a particular client.

Note that Cid will move from Hammerhead to Cape Caem by Chapter 8, and will continue upgrading weapons there.
