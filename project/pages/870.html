    EditExclusivity

    We learned shortly after E3 2016 that Dead Rising 4 is a timed exclusive to the Xbox One. After one year, it's possible we will be seeing the game on other platforms. This also means we could see it arrive on Steam, instead of simply being on the Windows 10 Store. The PC/Windows 10 Store exclusivity is expected to last a much shorter time, only 90 days.

    EditPossible Release Date

    A listing on the Xbox store lists Dead Rising 4 as releasing on December 6, 2016. This release date has not yet been confirmed by Microsoft themselves, but it does fit in to their holiday release window.
