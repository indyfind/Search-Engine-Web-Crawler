    IGN's complete Fallout 4 wiki guide provides a complete Walkthrough of every Location and Secret in the Commonwealth. Set in Boston, Massachusetts, Fallout 4 centers around an emerging inhabitant of Vault 111 as he or she awakens 200 years after the atomic war that destroyed much of the world's infrastructure.
