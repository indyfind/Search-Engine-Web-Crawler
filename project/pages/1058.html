    Final Fantasy XV Wiki Guide

    Edit Page    Last Edit: December 7, 2016 - 2 days 1 hour ago
    Released Platforms
    November 29, 2016 Playstation 4
    Xbox One
    Developer Publisher
    Square Enix Square Enix
    Official Sites
    Official US website

    Final Fantasy XV is a fantasy RPG originally announced at E3 2013. Originally slated for a September 30 release, the game has been delayed to November 29, 2016.

    Set in modern times but with a touch of medieval influences, the story focuses on Noctis Lucis Caelum, a prince on an epic journey from his home in the kingdom of Lucis. Noctis is joined on his journey by three companions, his friends Ignis Scienta, a melee fighter named Gladiolus Amicitia, and gun-wielding Prompto Argentum.

    Noctis is heir to the kingdom of his father Regis, but his peaceful life is shattered when the belligerent kingdom of Neflheim invades his realm. Against this backdrop of a world at war, Noctis Lucis Caelum and his closest friends embark on an epic journey.
